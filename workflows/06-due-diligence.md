# 06 — Due Diligence

Phase-gated checklist. Each phase has hard deliverables that must be cleared before the next stage.

## Phase A — Pre-LOI (concurrent with workflow 04)
Confirm nothing in the deal kills the thesis.
- [ ] Title search (preliminary) — material liens, easements
- [ ] Zoning confirmation — current use is conforming
- [ ] Environmental Phase I — order if not provided
- [ ] T-12 + rent roll tied to bank statements (sample)
- [ ] Major tenant credit (commercial) — D&B / 10-K review
- [ ] Insurance loss runs — last 3 years

## Phase B — LOI to PSA execution
Detailed legal & operational diligence.
- [ ] Title commitment + survey
- [ ] Property Condition Assessment (PCA) — engineer report
- [ ] Environmental Phase I final + Phase II if recommended
- [ ] Zoning letter from municipality
- [ ] Service contracts review (assignable vs. terminable)
- [ ] Estoppels from major tenants (commercial)
- [ ] SNDAs from major tenants (commercial)
- [ ] Lease abstracts (all leases for commercial; sample for multifamily)
- [ ] Property tax appeal history & re-assessment risk

## Phase C — PSA execution to close
Final confirmations and money movement prep.
- [ ] Title clear to close
- [ ] Survey signed off by lender + title
- [ ] Estoppels received and matched to abstracts
- [ ] Loan documents finalized
- [ ] Insurance binder issued effective close date
- [ ] Settlement statement reviewed and signed
- [ ] Wire instructions confirmed via callback to known number (NOT email)
